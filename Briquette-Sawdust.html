Briquette Sawdust


Sawdust charcoal briquette is made from hardwood sawdust. Sawdust as the main raw material in making briquettes, then is compressed into blocks of identical size and heated to become high quality charcoal briquettes. It is premium sawdust charcoal briquette which is produce little to none smoke, odor, or spark. Sawdust charcoal briquette is suitable for normal cooking and used in barbeque restaurant.
