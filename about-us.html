  COMPANY PROFILE


  CHARCOALINE, is a company under the name of CV Abimantrana Mega Nusantara was established in January 2022. The owner and production team have been experienced in the research and production of hardwood charcoal and coconut shell charcoal since 2017. The abundant raw materials in various regions in Indonesia are a great potential to be processed. We are committed to involving labor, natural resources, raw material suppliers, transportation from the surrounding environment, in order to create sustainable employment.

  The capacity of our Hardwood Charcoal Factory is capable of producing 120 tons per month, and for Coconut Shell Charcoal production 50 tons per month. The capacity can still increase according to demand, of course, assisted by fellow charcoal producers who have also trusted their quality.

  Besides exporting hardwood charcoal and coconut shell charcoal, we are also trusted to provide various types of briquettes as trader. The honesty factor is the main thing that we must always maintain in order to foster trust and comfort for each of our customers.
