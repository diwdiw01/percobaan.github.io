    Following international standards of production

    Hardwood Charcoal is usually produced by slow pyrolysis, the heating of wood or other substances in the absence of oxygen.

    Renewable environmentally friendly

    Made by burning coconut shells at high temperatures, this charcoal is free from harmful chemicals and burns cleaner and longer than traditional charcoal.

    Leading to a more sustainable and efficient process

    It has been carbonized and mixed with natural adhesives, making them not only the quickest and most consistent burners but also some of the cleanest on the market.

    Collected from a timber factory,
    chopstick factory or furniture factory

    As the main raw material in making briquettes, It is compressed into blocks of identical size and heated to become high quality charcoal briquettes.

    Famous for their quality, and increasingly in demand

    They have light beautiful ashes, consistently high calories and long burning time. No smoke, no sparks, no odors and burns evenly across the surface at high heat with a hot burning color.

      Frequently Asked Questions



    • Can I visit your factory ?

      Of course ! If you’re interested in seeing our production process, please let us know your expected arrival date and time, and we’ll arrange to pick you up at either Surabaya International Airport.

      Our factory is located in Jember, East Java. Our team will transport you to the factory, which is about a five-hour drive from the airport.

      Thank you for considering a visit to our factory!

    • Can you do charcoal ? derivatives with our brand's name ?

      Yes, we can produce charcoal and its derivatives with your brand and design.

      We focus on producing charcoal with high quality. However, some customers also ask us to handle their brand design process. The design process in our team will be charged a fee before the production process starts to determine the right size.

      Please contact us to get the layout for inner box and master box packaging, so that your designer can create the design easily and correctly.

      Thank you for considering our services for your charcoal needs..

    • What's about quality control ?

      We take quality control very seriously when producing hardwood charcoal, and coconut shell charcoal. Likewise, in providing all kinds of briquettes, it is in accordance with the specifications you have chosen.

      We have conducted CARSURIN certified tests to test our products for ash content, moisture, volatile matter, and other important parameters.

      You can also monitor and get information from us regularly from the beginning of the transaction, the production process, to the stuffing process. Or you can also hire an independent surveyor to update the information of your goods.

    • What's your payment terms ?

      We accept payment via TT bank transfer (Telegraph transfer) to our company account in Indonesia. Our preferred bank is Bank Mandiri (for any currency payment that will be converted in USD or IDR). Our Swift Bank Mandiri code is BMRIIDJA.

      All payments are made in accordance with the Invoice in the name of our company CV. Abimantrana Mega Nusantara

      We do not accept payments via Western Union, Paypal (except payment for samples & design fees), or other payment methods that cannot be accepted by banks.

      Our payment terms require a 50% down payment to start the production process of charcoal and its derivatives. Once we receive the down payment, we will start the production process, which takes approximately 20 working days (production process and packaging order) for all types of charcoal and its derivatives. After the container is shipped, we will prepare shipping documents, including Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, invoice, and packing list, which takes about 3-5 days.

      We will send you a copy of the B/L and COO, and you will transfer the remaining 50% payment to us. After receiving the payment, we will send you the original documents by DHL or telex release.

      We do not accept L/C for orders less 5 containers based on MOQ for each charcoal and its derivatives. However, we accept L/C at sight for all countries for larger orders. We also offer payment terms such as FOB, CNF, CIF, and EXW. Please contact us at sales@charcoaline.com or +62 821 399 484 68 (WhatsApp) if you have further questions or need clarification on our payment terms.

    • What's the production and delivery time ?

      For the first order with quantities according to the MOQ of each product ordered, including production and packaging orders, it takes 15 - 20 days. This lead time can be reduced if the order is continuous or when ordering a second order.

      We ship the charcoal from the port of Surabaya (Tanjung Perak, ID SUB), Indonesia. It takes 12-18 hours to track the container from the charcoal factory to the port. The distance can be covered by car in 5 hours, but trucks move more slowly.

      Please refer to the top of the FAQ to see the estimated time of arrival.

    • How to order ?

      To place an order, first, choose the charcoal item and specification that suits your needs.

      Next, contact us via Whatsapp at +62 821 399 484 68 or email us at sales@charcoaline.com. We will confirm the charcoal production date, availability, and issue your invoice.

      You can also visit our charcoal factory to see and test it. Please confirm the schedule in advance, as we also serve other clients.

    • What's your production capacity ?

      Our hardwood charcoal production capacity is 120 tons per month, and it can be increased according to orders. We use 21 kilns with 3 blowers to ensure a stable and efficient production process. As for our coconut shell charcoal production is 60 tons per month.

      For briquette supply, our capacity can reach 200 tons per month for coconut shell and bbq briquette respectively, while sawdust briquette is 150 tons per month.

    • How can I get a sample ?

      To request a sample, please contact us via Whatsapp at +62 821 399 484 68 or sales@charcoaline.com

      We will provide you with all the necessary details about our products including specifications, pricing and delivery options.

      We offer free samples that will be sent via DHL/FedEx/EMS/SDEK to ensure product quality. However, please note that shipping costs for sample products will be charged to customers.
